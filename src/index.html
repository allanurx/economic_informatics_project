What is Technology Transfer

Technology transfer refers to the process of sharing and disseminating technology, knowledge, and skills between different entities, such as between research institutions, universities, businesses, or countries. It involves the movement of valuable technical information and innovations from one place to another for commercial or societal benefit. This process often involves the licensing or sale of intellectual property rights, collaboration agreements, joint ventures, or other means of sharing knowledge.
